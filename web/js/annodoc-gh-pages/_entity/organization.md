References to human groups that have been established for a specific
purpose are annotated as `ORGANIZATION`.

<!-- details -->

Example:

~~~ ann
Google acquired YouTube.
T1 ORGANIZATION 0 6 Google
T2 ORGANIZATION 16 23 YouTube
~~~
