Leitklänge (Alexander Huber)

Leitklänge is a visualization of the patterns in the phonemic makeup of a poem. It visualizes vowel and consonant groups and distributions using easy-to-read colour highlighting. The visualization was inspired by Marc R. Plamondon's English Poetic Phonemics-project (2008/9) and extends it to include sound-based rhetorical devices such as alliteration, assonance, and consonance.
