To a young Lady who was going to India.

1 SHALL we once more then meet on Albion's coast,
2 Before, my dear, in India you're a toast?
3 There gilded pleasures wait your jet-black eyes,
4 And Asian youths for Scots Maria dies.
5 Yes! they may die — and die — and die again,
6 But ye's return and wed a Scottish swain,
7 Or wed him there.
