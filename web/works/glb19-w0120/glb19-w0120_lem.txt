the Rhine.

it be morn, and beauteous on the mountain brow
(hang with the cluster of the bend vine)
shine in the early light, when on the Rhine
we bound, and the white wave round the prow
in murmur part: — vary as we go,
lo! the wood open, and the rock retire,
as some grey convent-wall or glisten spire
'amid the bright landscape track unfold slow!
here dark, with furrow aspect, like despair,
frown the bleak cliff! there on the woodland side
the shadowy sunshine pour its stream tide;
while hope, enchant with the scene so fair,
count not the hour of a long summer day,
nor heed how fast the prospect wind away.
