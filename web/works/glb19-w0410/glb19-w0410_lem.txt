hourglass and bible.

look, christian, on thy bible, and that glass
that shed its sand through minute, hour, and day,
and year; it speak not, yet, methinks, it say,
to every human heart: so mortal pass
on to their dark and silent grave! alas
for man! a exile upon earth he stray,
weary, and wander through benight way;
today in strength, tomorrow like the grass
that withereth at his foot! — lift up thy head,
poor pilgrim, toil in this vale of tear;
that book declare who blood for thou be shed,
who die to give thou life; and though thy year
pass like a shade, point to thy death-bed,
out of the deep thy cry a angel hear,
and by his guide hand thy step to heaven be lead!
