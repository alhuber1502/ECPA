EPIGRAM VII.

1 I Swore I lov'd, and you believ'd,
2 Yet, trust me, we were both deceiv'd;
3 Tho' all I swore, was true.
4 I lov'd one gen'rous, good, and kind,
5 A form created in my mind;
6 And thought that form was you.
