    There oft is found an Avarice in Grief;
    And the wan Eye of Sorrow loves to gaze
    Upon the secret Hoard of treasur'd Woes.
    MASON.

    • RICHARD BRINSLEY SHERIDAN, Esq.

    SIR,

    EQUALLY induced by a just admiration of your talents, and a grateful sense of the distinction your praise has given to those you were pleased to find in me, permit me to solicit your further indulgence to this little production; and be assured, its greatest value in my eyes is, that it serves to convey those acknowledgments, with which I have the honour to remain,

    SIR,
    Your most obliged, Obedient humble servant, SOPHIA LEE.
