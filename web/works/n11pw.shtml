  • dedication

    • To the CHURCH and CONGREGATION assembling in Brattle-Street, the following ELEGY, Sacred to the MEMORY of their late Reverend and Worthy PASTOR, DR. SAMUEL COOPER, is, with the greatest Sympathy, most respectfully inscribed by their Obedient, Humble Servant,

    PHILLIS PETERS.
    Boston,Jan. 1784
