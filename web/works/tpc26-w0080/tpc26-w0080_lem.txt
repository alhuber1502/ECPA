a account of the burn of Bachelor’s hall

to the publisher of the Pennsylvania magazine

fair Venus so often be miss’d from the sky,
and Bacchus as frequent absent likewise,
that the synod begin to inquire out the reason,
suspect the culprit be plot of treason.
at length it be find they have open’d a ball,
at a place by the mortal call’d bachelor’ hall;
where Venus disclos’d ev’ry fun she canthink of,
and Bacchus make nectar for mortal todrink of.
Jove high displease at such riotous do
send time to reduce the whole building to ruin.
but time be so slack with his trace and dash
that Jove in a passion consume it to ash.
