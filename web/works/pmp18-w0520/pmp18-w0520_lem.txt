adriani morientis ad animam suam.

imitate.

poor little, pretty, flutter thing,
 must we no long live together? 
and do thou prune thy tremble wing,
 to take thy flight thou know not whither? 

thy humorous vein, thy please folly
 lie all neglect, all forget:
 and pensive, waver, melancholy,
 thou dread and hope thou know not what.
