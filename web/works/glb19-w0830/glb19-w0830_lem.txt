stanza for music.

i trust the happy hour will come,
that shall to peace thy breast restore;
and that we two, belove friend,
shall one day meet to part no more.

it grieve i most, that part thus,
all my soul feel i dare not speak;
and when i turn i from thy sight,
the tear in silence wet my cheek.

yet i look forward to the time,
that shall each wound of sorrow heal;
when i may press thou to my heart,
and tell thou all that now i feel.
