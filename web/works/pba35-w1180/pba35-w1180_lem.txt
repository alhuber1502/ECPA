to mrs. Frances-Arabella Kelly, with a present of fruit.

by the same. 

though the plumb, and the peach, with Apollo conspire,
 to present you their softness, and sweetness, and fire;
 their aid be in vain; for what can they do,
 but blush, and confess they self vanquish by you? 
where virtue and wit with such quality blend,
 what mortal, what goddess, will dare to contend?
