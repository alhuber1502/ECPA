EPIGRAM ON SAID OCCASION,

1 O Death, hadst thou but spar'd his life,
2 Whom we, this day, lament!
3 We freely wad exchang'd the wife,
4 An' a' been weel content.
5 Ev'n as he is, cauld in his graff,
6 The swap we yet will do't;
7 Tak thou the Carlin's carcase aff,
8 Thou'se get the saul o' boot.
