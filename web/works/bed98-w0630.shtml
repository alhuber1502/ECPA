ATTENDANCE UPON RELIGIOUS INSTITUTIONS.

1 I'LL bless Jehovah's glorious name,
2 Whose goodness heaven and earth proclaim,
3 With every morning's light;
4 And at the close of ev'ry day,
5 To him my cheerful homage pay,
6 Who guards me thro' the night.
7 Then in his churches to appear,
8 And pay my humble worship there,
9 Shall be my sweet employ;
10 The day that saw my Saviour rise,
11 Shall dawn on my delighted eyes,
12 With every sacred joy.
[Page 211]
13 With grateful sorrow in my breast,
14 I'll celebrate the dying feast,
15 Of my departing Lord;
16 And while his perfect love I view,
17 His bright example I'll pursue,
18 And meditate his word.
