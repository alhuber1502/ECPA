to the right honourable the earl of Chesterfield, on his being installead knight of the garter.

by the same.

these trophy, stanhope, of the lovely dame,
once the bright object of a monarch flame,
who with such just propriety can wear,
as thou, the darling of the gay and fair?
see every friend to wit, politeness, love,
with one consent thy sovereign choice approve!
and live Plantagenet her voice to join,
herself, and garter, both be sure thy.
