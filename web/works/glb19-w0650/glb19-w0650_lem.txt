Wardour castle.

if rich design of sumptuous art may please,
or nature lofty view, august and old,
stranger! behold this spread scene; — behold
this amphitheatre of age tree,
that solemn wave above thou, and around
darken the tower hill! do thou complain
that thou shall cope with penury or pain,
or sigh to think what pleasure may be find
amid such wide possession! — pause awhile;
imagine thou do see the sick man smile;
see the pale exile, that in yonder dome,
safe from the wasteful storm, have find a home;
and thank the giver of all good, that lend
to the humane, retire, beneficent,
the power to bless. nor lift thy heart elate,
if such domain be thy; but emulate
the fair example, and those deed, that rise
like holy incense waft to the sky;
those deed that shall sustain the conscious soul,
when all this empty world have perish, like a scroll!
