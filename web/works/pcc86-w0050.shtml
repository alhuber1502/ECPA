A DREAM.

1 A Peaceful morn the sweets of spring convey,
2 A radiant beam to usher in the day;
3 Soft as the breeze in whisper seem'd to say,
4 In angel mildness begg'd me to obey.
5 Thus rais'd to hope; but glides my dream away,
6 Her form transparent, brighter than the day.
7 What joy sublime, what innocence around,
8 With kind benevolence and myrtle crown'd;
9 Still in my ear melodious sounds I hear,
10 Ecstatic themes of her eternal year.
