accidental reconciliation

from the castle in the air to the little corner of the world

in the region of cloud where the whirlwind arise
my castle of fancy be build,
the turret reflect the blue of the sky,
and the window with sunbeam be gild.

the rainbow sometime in its beautiful state
enamel the mansion around,
and the figure that fancy in the cloud can create
supply i with garden and ground.

i have grotto and fountain and orange-tree grove,
i have all that enchantment have tell,
i have sweet shady walk for the god and their love,
i have mountain of coral and gold.

but a storm that i feel not have rise and roll’d,
while wrap in a slumber i lay,
and when i look’d out in the morning, behold,
my castle be carry away.

it pass’d over river and valley and grove,
the world it be all in my view,
i think of my friend, of their fate, and their love,
and often, full often, of you.

at length it come over a beautiful scene
that nature in silence have make,
the place be but small, but be sweet serene,
and checquer’d with sunshine and shade.

i gaz’d, and i envy, with painful good will,
and grow tir’d of my seat in the air,
when all on a sudden my castle stand still
as if some attraction be there.

like a lark from the sky it come flutter down,
and plac’d i exact in view,
when who shall i meet, in this charm retreat,
this corner of calmness but you.

delight to find you in honour and ease
i feel neither sorrow nor pain,
and the wind come fair, i ascend the air,
and go back with my castle again.

how strange be the change that torture we here,
where tempest and hurricane reign,
but the friendship that suffer be sure sincere
what e’er be the cause of the pain.
