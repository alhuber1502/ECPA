extempore. on a draw of the countess of hertford, now duchess of somerset.

this piece to late time when show,
 Hertford, shall dignify your own;
 where as a visitor you come,
 just show yourself, and leave your name. 
so, prior say, some year ago,
 Apelles leave his name at co.  
learn hence, you nymph of britain isle,
 how Hertford write, and mark her style.
