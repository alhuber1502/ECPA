sonnet. [how sweet be love first gentle sway]

how sweet be love first gentle sway,
when crown with flower he soft smile!
his blue eye fraught with tearful wile,
where beam of tender transport play:
hope lead he on his airy way,
and faith and fancy still beguile —
faith quick tangle in her toil —
fancy, who magic form so gay
the fair deceiver self deceive —
"how sweet be love first gentle sway;"
never will that heart he bid to grieve
from sorrow soft enchantment stray —
never — till the god exult in his art,
relentless frown and wing the envenom dart,
