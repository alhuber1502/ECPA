Love.

1 Love, like Original Sin, in all does dwell,
2 Fools sighs in private, and the Witty tell;
3 Boast they'r fond Passions in repeated Rhymes,
4 That other Reigning Mischief of the Times:
5 The Learn'd asham'd to own their Amorous Pain,
6 Vent the warm Raptures in a Pious strain,
7 Sigh, Languish, Die, (tho' for a Mortal fair,)
8 In Lays Divine, like Quarles and Arwaker.
