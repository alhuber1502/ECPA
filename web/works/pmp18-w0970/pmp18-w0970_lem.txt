in the same [chaucer] style.

fair Susan do her wife-heed well menteine,
 algates assault sore by letchour tweine;
 now, and i read aright that ancient song,
 old be the paramour, the dame full young. 
have thilke same tale in other guise be tell;
 have they be young (pardie) and she be old;
 that, by saint Kit, have wrought much sore trial:
 full merveillous, i wot, be swilk denial.
