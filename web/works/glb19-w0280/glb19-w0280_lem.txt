music.

o harmony! thou tender nurse of pain,
if that thy note sweet magic ever can heal
grief which the patient spirit oft may feel,
oh! let i listen to thy song again;
till memory her fair tint shall bring;
hope wake with bright eye, and listen seem
with smile to think on some delightful dream,
that wave over the charm sense its gladsome wing!
for when thou lead all thy soothe strain
more smooth along, the silent passion meet
in one suspend transport, sad and sweet;
and nought but sorrow soft touch remain;
that, when the transitory charm be over,
just wake a tear, and then be feel no more.
