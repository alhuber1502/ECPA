to a lady, who value herself on speak her mind in a blunt manner, which she call be sincere.

well you sincerity display,
 a virtue wondrous rare! 
nor value, though the world shall say,
 you be rude, so you be sincere. 

to be sincere, then, give i leave;
 and i will frank own,
 since you but this one virtue have,
 it be better you have none.
