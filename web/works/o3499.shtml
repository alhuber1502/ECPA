    • THE CAMPAIGN, A POEM, To His GRACE the DUKE of MARLBOROUGH.

    By Mr. ADDISON.

    — Rheni pacator & Istri.
    Omnis in hoc Uno variis discordia cessit
    Ordinibus; laetatur Eques, plauditque Senator,
    Votaque Patricio certant Plebeia favori.
    Claud. de Laud. Stilic.
