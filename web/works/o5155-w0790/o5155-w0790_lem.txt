write at a ferme ornee near Birmingham; august 7th, 1749. 

by the same.

it be nature here bid please scene arise,
and wise give they cynthio, to revise:
to veil each blemish; brighten every grace;
yet still preserve the lovely parent face.

how well the bard obey, each valley tell;
these lucid stream, gay mead, and lonely cell;
where modest art in silence lurk conceal:
while nature shine, so graceful reveal,
that she triumphant claim the total plan;
and, with fresh pride, adopt the work of man.
