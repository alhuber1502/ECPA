On her BIRTH-DAY, Being the 11th of December.

1 Why this day's shorter than the rest,
2 A modern bard full well has guest.
3 The sun who shines the year about,
4 And ev'ry lesser light puts out,
5 This day submits, and will not rise,
6 But lends his rays to Stella's eyes.
