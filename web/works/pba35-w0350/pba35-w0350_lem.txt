say, worsdeal, where you learn the art
 to paint the goodness of the heart
 the flatter taint let other prize;
 you call the soul into the eye:
 there we the various virtue trace
 of churchil, and Godolphin race. 
thrice happy Pelham, to who arm
 be destine never-fading charm!
