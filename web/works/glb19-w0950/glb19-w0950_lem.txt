CADLAND, Southampton river.

if ever sea-maid, from her coral cave,
beneath the hum of the great surge, have love
to pass delight from her green abode,
and, seat on a summer bank, to sing
no earthly music; in a spot like this,
the bard may feign he hear she, as she dry
her golden hair, yet drip from the main,
in the slant sunbeam.
so the pensive bard
may image, warm by this enchant scene,
the ideal form; but though such thing be not,
he who have ever feel a thought refine;
he who have wander on the sea of life,
form delightful vision of a home
of beauty and repose; he who have love,
with filial warmth his country, will not pass
without a look of more than tenderness
on all the scene; from where the pensile birch
bend on the bank, amid the cluster group
of the dark holly; to the woody shore
that steal diminish, to the distant spire
of Hampton, crown the long lucid wave.
white in the sun, beneath the forest-shade,
full shine the frequent sail, like vanity,
as she go onward in her glitter trim,
amid the glance of life transient morn,
call on all to view she!
vectis there,
that slope its greensward to the lambent wave,
and show through soft haze its wood and dome,
with grey saint Catherine creep to the sky,
seem like a modest maid, who charm the more
conceal half her beauty.
to the east,
proud, yet complacent, on its subject realm,
with mast innumerable throng, and hull
see indistinct, but formidable, mark
albion vast fleet, that, like the impatient storm,
wait but the word to thunder and flash death
on he who dare approach to violate
the shore and live scene that smile secure
beneath its dragon-watch!
long may they smile!
and long, majestic Albion (while the sound
from east to west, from Albis to the po,
of dark contention hurtle), may thou rest,
as calm and beautiful this sylvan scene
look on the refluent wave that steal below.
