art and nature.

the bridge between CLIFTON and Leigh wood.

frown ever opposite, the angel cry,
who, with a earthquake might and giant hand,
sever these rive rock, and bid they stand
sever for ever! the vast ocean-tide,
leave its roar without at his command,
shrink, and beneath the wood through the green land
go gentle murmur on, so to deride
the frown barrier that its force defy!
but art, high over the trail smoke below
of sea-bound steamer, on yond summit head
sit muse; and where scarce a wander crow
sail over the chasm, in think a highway lead;
conquer, as by a arrow from a bow,
the scene lone genius by her elfin-thread.
