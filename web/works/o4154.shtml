  • TO THE READER.

    • TO THE READER.

    THE following lines were lately wrote in a fit of illness, without any in tention of ever troubling the public with them; but some very incorrect copies having been dis pers'd,[Page vi] unknown to the author occasion'd this edition.

    The writer has look'd on life too long, and suffer'd too much in it, to be anxious for the even of these rhymes: they were wrote neither for profit, nor re putation; if he gets either by them, its more than he expected or if he offends, its what he never design'd.

    Bath,Feb. 22, 1751.
