anniversary of american independence

July 4 tune rule Britannia

hail great republic of the world,
the rise empire of the west,
where fam’d Columbus, of mighty mind inspir’d,
give tortur’d Europe scene of rest.
be thou for ever, for ever, great and free,
the land of love and liberty.

2

beneath thy spread mantling vine,
beside thy flow’ry grove and spring,
and on thy lofty, thy lofty, mountain brow,
may all thy son and fair one sing,
be thou for ever, for ever, great and free,
the land of love and liberty.

3

from (thou) thou may hate discord fly,
with all her dark, her dreary train,
and while thy mighty, thy mighty, Waters roll
may heart endear concord reign.
be thou for ever, for ever, great and free
the land of love and liberty

4

far as the vast atlantic pour
its load wave to human sight,
there may thy starry, thy starry; standard shine,
the constellation of thy right.
be thou for ever, for ever, great and free
the land of love and liberty.

5

may age as they rise proclaim
the glory of thy natal day,
and restless Europe, from thy example learn
to love, to rule, and to obey.
be thou for ever, for ever, great and free
the land of love and liberty.

6

let laureate sing their birthday ode,
or how their deathlike thunder’s hurl’d,
it be we the charter, the charter, we alone
to sing the birthday of a world
be thou for ever, for ever, great and free
the land of love and liberty.
