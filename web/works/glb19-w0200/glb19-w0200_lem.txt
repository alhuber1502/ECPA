absence.

there be strange music in the stir wind,
when lower the autumnal eve, and all alone
to the dark wood cold covert thou be go,
who ancient tree on the rough slope recline
rock, and at time scatter their tress seré.
if in such shade, beneath their murmur,
thou late have pass the happy hour of spring,
with sadness thou will mark the fade year;
chief if one, with who such sweet at morn
or evening thou have share, afar shall stray.
o spring, return! return, auspicious may!
but sad will be thy come, and forlorn,
if she return not with thy cheer ray,
who from these shade be go, far, far away.
