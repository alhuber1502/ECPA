song. [life be a vary, bright illusion]

life a vary, bright illusion,
joy and sorrow — light and shade;
turn from sorrow dark suffusion,
catch the pleasure ere they fade.

fancy paint with hue unreal,
smile of bliss, and sorrow mood;
if they both be but ideal,
why reject the seem good?

hence! no more! it be wisdom call you,
bid you court time be present aid;
the future trust not — hope enthrall you,
"catch the pleasure ere they fade."
