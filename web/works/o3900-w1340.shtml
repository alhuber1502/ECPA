GRACE before MEAT.

I.
1 PARENT of Good, whose plenteous Grace
2 O'er all thy Creatures flows,
3 Humbly we ask thy Pow'r to bless
4 The Food thy Love bestows.
II.
5 Thy Love provides the sober Feast:
6 A Second Gift impart,
7 Give us with Joy our Food to taste
8 And with a Single Heart.
III.
9 Let it for Thee new Life afford,
10 For Thee our Strength repair,
11 Blest by thine all-sustaining Word,
12 And sanctify'd by Prayer.
IV.
13 Thee let us taste; nor toil below
14 For perishable Meat:
15 The Manna of thy Love bestow,
16 Give us thy Flesh to eat.
[Page 216]
V.
17 Life of the World, our Souls to feed
18 Thyself descend from high!
19 Grant us of Thee the Living Bread
20 To eat, and never die!
