stanza. [how smooth that lake expand its ample breast!]

how smoothe that lake expand its ample breast!
where smile in soften glow the summer sky:
how vast the rock that over its surface rest!
how wild the scene its wind shore supply!

now down the western steep slow sink the sun,
and paint with yellow gleam the tuft wood:
while here the mountain-shadow, broad and dun,
sweep over the crystal mirror of the flood.

Mark how his splendour tip with partial light
those shatter battlement! that on the brow
of yond bold promontory burst to sight
from over the wood that dark spread below.

in the soft blush of light be reflect power,
the ridgy rock, the wood that crown its steep,
the illumine battlement, and dark tower,
on the smooth wave in tremble beauty sleep.

but lo! the sun recall his fervid ray,
and cold and dim, the watery vision fail;
while over yond cliff, who point crag decay,
mild evening draw her thin empurple veil!

how sweet that strain of melancholy horn!
that float along the slow ebb wave;
and up the far-receding mountain bear,
return a die close from echo cave!

hail! shadowy form of still, expressive eve!
your pensive grace steal on my heart,
bid all the fine-attuned emotion live,
and fancy all her lovely dream impart.
