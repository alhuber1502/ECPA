A HYMN FOR NEW YEAR'S DAY.

1 AGAIN time's ever fleeting hand
2 Points to the finish'd year;
3 It's moments with past ages stand,
4 Nor more for me appear.
5 Say has improvement mark'd your flight,
6 Seasons in mercy given?
7 Does the recording angel write
8 His testimony, heaven?
9 For tho' from me for ever flown,
10 Unchang'd you awful mount;
11 And angels smile, or fiends you frown
12 Upon my last account.
[Page 217]
13 Then as the opening dawn I hail,
14 Of time to me renew'd;
15 Be what I do and where I fail,
16 Impartially review'd.
17 And while th' important moments pass,
18 And time again revolves;
19 Spirit of power and heavenly grace,
20 Assist my weak resolves.
21 From each imperfect virtuous part,
22 Then purer good shall spring;
23 From errors past, a contrite heart,
24 Sainted repentance bring.
