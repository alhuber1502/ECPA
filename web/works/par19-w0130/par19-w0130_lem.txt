sunset.

soft over the mountain purple brow
meek twilight draw her shadow grey:
from tuft wood and valley low,
light magic colour steal away.
yet still, amid the spread gloom,
resplendent glow the western wave,
that roll over neptune coral cave,
a zone of light on evening dome.
on this lone summit let i rest,
and view the form to fancy dear,
until on the ocean darken breast
the star of evening tremble clear;
or the moon pale orb appear,
throw her line of radiance wide,
far over the lightly-curling tide,
that seem the yellow sand to chide.
no sound over silence now prevail,
save of the die wave below,
or sailor song bear on the gale,
or oar at distance strike slow.
so sweet! so tranquil! may my evening ray
set to this world — and rise in future day.
