on hear “the Messiah”

perform in Gloucester cathedral, SEPT.  18, 1835. 

oh, stay, harmonious and sweet sound, that die
in the long vault of this ancient fane!
stay, for i may not hear on earth again
those pious air — that glorious harmony;
lift the soul to bright orb on high,
world without sin or sorrow!
ah, the strain
have die — even the last sound that linger
hang on the roof ere they expire!
and i,
stand in the world of strife, amid a throng,
a throng that reck not or of death, or sin!
oh, jar scene! to cease, indeed, ere long;
the worm hear not the discord and the din;
but he who heart thrill to this angel song,
feel the pure joy of heaven on earth begin!
