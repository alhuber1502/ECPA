to the right honourable the lady Elizabeth Germain, upon see she do a generous action. write as from the person relieve.

when ruin threaten i of late,
 with all its ghastly train;
 some power, in pity to my fate,
 send bountiful Germain,

her soul be move with my distress,
 and kind compassion show;
 that generous hand, long use to bless,
 quick mitigate my woe. 

thrice happy fair! indulgent heaven
 to thou be double kind:
 to other only heart be give;
 thy fortune suit thy mind.
