CHISWICK.

by the same.

the potent lord, that this bright villa plan,
exhibit here a paradise regain;
whatever of verdure have hill, lawn, or wood,
whatever of splendour, building, flower, or flood,
whatever of fruit the tree, of bird the air,
in blissful union be collect here:
all with such harmony dispose, as show,
that in the midst the tree of knowledge grow.
