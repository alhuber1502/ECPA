Rondeau. [soft as yond silver ray, that sleep]

soft as yond silver ray, that sleep
upon the ocean tremble tide;
soft as the air, that light sweep
yond sail, that swell in stately pride:

soft as the surge steal note,
that die along the distant shore,
or warble strain, that sink remote —
so soft the sigh my bosom pour!

true as the wave to cynthia ray,
true as the vessel to the breeze,
true as the soul to music sway,
or music to venetian sea:

soft as yond silver beam, that sleep
upon the ocean tremble breast:
so soft, so true, fond love shall weep,
so soft, so true, with thou shall rest.
