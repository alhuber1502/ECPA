HON. miss Mercer. — HOPNER.

oh! hide those tempt eye, that faultless form,
those look with feeling and with nature warm;
the neck, the softly-swelling bosom hide,
nor, wanton gale, blow the light vest aside;
for who, when beauty more than life excite
silent applause, can gaze without delight!
but innocence, enchant maid, be thy;
thy eye in liquid light unconscious shine;
and may thy breast no other feeling prove,
than those of sympathy and mutual love!
