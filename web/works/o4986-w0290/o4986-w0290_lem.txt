this bubble stream not uninstructive flow,
 nor idle loiter to its destine main,
 each flower it feed that on its margin grow,
 and bid thou blush, who day be spend in vain. 

nor void of moral, though unheeded, glide
 time be current steal on with silent haste;
 for lo! each fall sand his folly chide,
 who let one precious moment run to waste.
