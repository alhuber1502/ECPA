Kinross-shire

To Miss A. H—.

1 SHort was their stay, of high descent they sprung,
2 Two beaut'ous*
* Miss —, and Miss —.
nymphs — and thus they sweetly sung:
3 Peace o'er each scene — peace o'er the pleasant fields;
4 No shades! to me — more pleasure ever yields.
5 O'er all thy minds — the passions calmly flow;
6 As Leven's! stream tranquil — to whence we go.
