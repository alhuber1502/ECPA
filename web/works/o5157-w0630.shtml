Another.

By the Same.
1 SUE venal Belinda to grant you the blessing
2 As Jove courted Danae, or vain's your addressing;
3 For love, she asserts, all that's generous inspires,
4 And therefore rich tokens of love she requires.
5 Such suitors as nothing but ardours are boasting,
6 Will ne'er reach Elysium, but ever be coasting,
7 Like pennyless ghosts deny'd passage by Charon,
8 They'll find, without fee, unrelenting the fair one.
9 But give me the nymph not ungrateful to wooing,
10 Who love pays with love, and caresses with cooing,
11 By whom a true heart is accepted as sterling,
12 And Cupid alone makes her lover her darling.
