dirge of Nelson.

toll Nelson knell! a soul more brave
never triumph on the green-sea wave!
sad over the hero honour grave,
toll Nelson knell!

the ball of death unerring fly;
his cheek have lose its ardent hue;
he sink, amid his gallant crew!
toll Nelson knell!

yet lift, brave chief, thy die eye;
hark! loud huzza around thou rise;
aloft the flag of conquest fly!
the day be win!

the day be win — peace to the brave!
but while the joyous streamer wave,
we will think upon the victor grave!
peace to the brave!
