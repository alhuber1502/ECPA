the new covenant

from the castle in the air to the little corner of the world.

the god that Moses write about
be one that i refuse,
he for his choose people take
the disobedient Jew.

their country often he lay waste.
their little one he slay;
but i have show a better taste
in choose y, o, u.

then be you gay, or be you grave,
or wheresoe’er you be,
mind this command, that “thou shall have
no other god than i.”

all idol thou shall put away
thy eye no evil see,
and if thou’lt love the lord thy god
thy lord god will love thou.
