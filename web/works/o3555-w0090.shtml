On a LADY's WRITING.

1 HER even lines her steady temper show;
2 Neat as her dress, and polish'd as her brow;
3 Strong as her judgment, easy as her air;
4 Correct though free, and regular though fair:
5 And the same graces o'er her pen preside
6 That form her manners and her footsteps guide.
