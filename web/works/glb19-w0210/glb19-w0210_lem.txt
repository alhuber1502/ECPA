who be that gentle voice, that, whisper sweet,
promise methinks long day of bliss sincere!
soothe it steal on my delude ear,
most like soft music, that may sometime cheat
thought dark and droop! it be the voice of hope.
of love, and social scene, it seem to speak,
of truth, of friendship, of affection meek;
that, oh! poor friend, may to life downward slope
lead we in peace, and bless our late hour.
ah i! the prospect sadden as she sing;
loud on my startle ear the death-bell ring;
chill darkness wrap the pleasurable bower,
while horror, point to yond breathless clay,
"no peace be thy," exclaim, "away, away!"
