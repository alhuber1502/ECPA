and always be sincere,
i will not wish for heaven above,
my heaven will be here.

though many country i have see,
and more may chance to see,
my little corner of the world
be half the world to i.

the other half, as you may guess,
America contain,
and thus between they i possess
the whole world for my pain.

I’m then content with my lot,
i can no happy be,
for neither world, i’m sure, have get
so rich a man as i.

then send no fiery chariot down
to take i off from hence,
but leave i on my heavenly ground —
— this prayer be common sense.

let other choose another plan,
i mean no fault to find,
the true theology of man
