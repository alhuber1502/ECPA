BOOK IV. Ep. 78.

1 With lace bedizen'd comes the man,
2 And I must dine with lady Anne.
3 A silver service loads the board,
4 Of eatables a slender hoard.
5 "Your pride, and not your victuals spare;
6 "I came to dine, and not to stare.
